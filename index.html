Our Story

Sky Land Middle East LLC is a distinguished real estate company with over two decades of excellence in the UAE property market. Founded on the principles of integrity, expertise, and unwavering client dedication, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in property acquisition and investment.

Our deep understanding of the Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Ras Al Khaimah markets enables us to provide tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. Whether you’re seeking your dream home, an investment property, or commercial space, our team of certified professionals is committed to guiding you through every step of the journey.

With extensive partnerships across the region’s leading developers and a proven track record of successful transactions, Sky Land Middle East LLC continues to set the standard for premium real estate services in the UAE.

About Raed Hamze

Founder & CEO – Sky Land Middle East Properties (Abu Dhabi) & Luxury Sky Land Properties (Dubai)

With over 21 years of experience in the UAE real estate market, Raed Hamze has established Sky Land as a trusted and respected brand known for professionalism and client commitment.

As a Certified Real Estate Instructor at ThinkProp Real Estate Training Institute, he plays a key role in elevating the industry through broker licensing and renewal programs. His credentials include CIPS, CRB, SRS, ABR, RERA, and ADREC certifications, reflecting his dedication to continuous learning and excellence.

Recognized with multiple industry honors including the Aldar Best Professional Conduct Award, ADREC & ThinkProp Certificates of Appreciation, and accolades from Bayut, Property Finder, and the Arabian Property Awards Raed remains committed to advancing real estate professionalism across the UAE.
